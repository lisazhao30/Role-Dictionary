Role

Write the name of the role you'd like to add.
A role can be a work or education position, a societal role, or a position in an organization (e.g. student, sister, volunteer).

Definition

What is the definition of your role?
E.g. the definition of "student" is someone who is studying at a school.

Rights

What are the rights of your role?
A right is something an individual/role has the moral or legal entity to obtain (e.g. a student has the right to be respected by their peers).

Responsibilities

What are the key responsibilities of your role?
A responsibility is something, such as a task, that your role must do (e.g. a student has to attend class).
